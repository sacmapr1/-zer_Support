Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What kind of math problems can Cozer solve?

Cozer can handle a wide range of math problems, from algebra and calculus to geometry and word problems, thanks to its powerful AI backend.

Does the app work offline?

No. Cozer requires an active internet connection to send your problem to our secure AI servers for analysis and solution.

Is my data and privacy protected?

Yes. We take your privacy seriously. The problems you submit are used only to generate a solution and are not stored long-term or linked to your personal identity. For full details, please read our Privacy Policy.
